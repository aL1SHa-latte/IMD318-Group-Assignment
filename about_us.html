ABOUT US

Our Story

Located in Tanah Merah, Kelantan, Atas Cafe is a popular spot with a varied cuisine and cozy atmosphere. We created an elegant Asian-fusion café design with hotel-style interior design. Popular with residents and tourists of all ages, our café was founded in 2020 and offers a wide selection of main courses, beverages, and desserts. In Kelantan, we now have three café branchs; two more are in Kota Bharu. Additionally, our café offers customers spacious areas, delicious meals, and Instagram-worthy spots for OOTD photos.
